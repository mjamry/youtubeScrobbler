Have you ever wondered if there is a simpler way to create a playlist and listen to the music from youtube ?
Here it is - a new simple and user friendly music player.
It also allows you to save your listening history on your last.fm account.


Music player
it is an new and improved music player so you can easily listen to youtube music.
